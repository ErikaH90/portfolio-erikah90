About me

Language teacher pursuing frontend development.

I’m a certified Swedish teacher with years of classroom experience, where I began creating my own learning materials for SFI students. Over time, I started asking a simple question: how could these resources reach more learners?
With a strong passion for both writing and visual design, creativity and problem solving, I realized only one piece was missing to make that happen—code. Drawn to the structure and logic of grammar, I found the transition into HTML and CSS both natural and exciting. The same patterns and rules that shape language also exist in code, and I quickly felt at home connecting the two.
By combining my skills, I built a website for immigrants learning Swedish—bringing together pedagogy, design and development into one cohesive project.
My curiosity didn’t stop there. I’m currently studying Front End Development at Medieinstitutet, where I’ve expanded my knowledge across modern frameworks, programming languages, API development and a box of tools usable for professional development.
This fall, I’m eager to take the next step and apply my skills in a LIA internship from September 21, 2026 to March 19, 2027.
